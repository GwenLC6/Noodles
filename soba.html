Cold Soba Dish

November 24, 2020 by Gwendoline

Choose 100% buckwheat noodles for a gluten-free option!

1. Up to 24 hours in advance, marinate your meat of choice (strips of beef work best) with a dash of salt, soy sauce, garlic and pepper. If you have some, add miso paste.

2. Boil soba noodles in a pot of salted water.

3. Once nearly cooked, drain in a colander and submerge in cold or icy water.

4. On high heat, sear the strips of meat in oil. Add any vegetables and scramble an egg, if desired.

5. Serve noodles with beef, egg and vegetables. Add soy sauce and/or vinegars to dip.
