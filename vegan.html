One-Wok Vegan Stir Fry

November 24, 2020 by Gwendoline

This versatile dish is a sure one-pot favourite. Makes 2:

1. Toss half an onion and 2 smashed cloves of garlic in cooking oil on medium heat.

2. Brown half a pack of cubed, pressed tofu in the pan on both sides.

3. Once browned, add vegetables of your choice (sliced or diced bell peppers, string beans, leafy greens) and stir.

4. Add a half a teaspoon of curry powder and a tablespoon of light soy sauce, a dash of turmeric and other cupboard seasons (garlic granules, black pepper, salt, sesame oil).

5. Immediately, add the packet of noodles. Stir until cooked.

6. Serve immediately.
