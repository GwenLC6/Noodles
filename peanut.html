Sweet and Sour Peanut Butter Noodles

November 24, 2020 by Gwendoline

A saucy take on that leftover jar of PB in your fridge... Makes 2:

1. Simmer green onions, garlic and chilis/chili oil.

2. On medium heat, toss vegetables and/or meat into the pan. Once nearly cooked, lower heat.

3. Place noodles and water into a new pot or pan, turning up the heat. Once nearly cooked, place noodles into the pan with the vegetables and/or meat.

4. Microwave peanut/almond butter, light soy sauce, maple syrup/honey, sesame oil, and a dash of water in a bowl - ratios of your choosing.

5. Pour the sauce onto the noodles, adding salt, pepper and chili to taste.

6. Dice nuts to sprinkle on top.
