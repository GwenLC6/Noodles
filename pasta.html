Spicy Pasta Fusion

November 24, 2020 by Gwendoline

No noodles? No worries...

1. Brown half a pack of pancetta, bacon or other fatty diced meat in an oiled pan, 9-10 minutes. Take off the heat.

2. Boil desired amount of spaghetti (around 200 grams) in a salted pot of water.

3. Meanwhile, mix a tablespoon of the starchy pasta water, a tablespoon of chili sauce (chili garlic sauce, sriracha), a tablespoon of miso sauce and a teaspoon of oyster sauce in a separate bowl. Add a dash of soy sauce to taste.

4. Beat in 2 eggs into the sauce, adding salt and pepper as desired.

5. Put bacon pan back on a low heat.

6. Remove pasta from water and add to the pan, swirling in the sauce. Be careful not to scramble the eggs!

7. Grate as much cheese (parmesan, preferably) onto the dish as desired, before plating.

8. Add spring onions, pepper, salt to taste.
