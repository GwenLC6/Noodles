Hearty Soup Noodle

November 24, 2020 by Gwendoline

Cold? Tired? Sick? Bored? Here's a slow-cook recipe! If you're in a rush, jump to step 9.

1. Heat oil in a large heavy pot over medium heat. Season short ribs or chicken with salt and cook, turning occasionally, until well browned on all sides. This could take 10+ minutes. Transfer to a platter as you go.

2. Simmer green onions, garlic, and ginger until brown. If you have these seasonings, add star anise, cinnamon, cloves, peppercorns until fragrant.

3. Add boiling water into the pot together with the meat from the platter. Simmer on a very low heat. If you are using beef, simmer for as long as you wish - you can do this overnight if you wish, switching the heat off at night and simmering again in the morning.

4. When you are ready to cook the rest of the dish, heat butter or oil in another pot on medium heat, adding 2-3 chopped onions and salt. Brown. This might take over 30 minutes.

5. Remove meat and add to the pot with onions.

6. Strain the broth and discard solids. Add broth to the pot with meat and onions.

7. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for about 30 minutes. Add vinegar and soy sauce to taste.

8. Add green onions, noodles. Serve when ready.

9. Pour boiling water into a pot and add any flavourings you have: a flavour packet, star anise, peppercorns, stock cube/powder, salt, a chopped onion.

10. Add noodles and any vegetables and thin slices of meat. It should be fully cooked in under 10 minutes.
